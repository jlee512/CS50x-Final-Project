Introduction

\nBlessed with a mild climate, golden beaches and lush coastal native bush, the Abel Tasman Coast Track has it all.\n
\n

Highlights

\n
\n
    \n
  • Visit golden sand beaches.
    • \n
    \n
  • Discover Cleopatra\'s Pool - \xc2\xa0natural rock pool with a moss-lined waterslide.
    • \n
    \n
  • Admire the beautiful inlet to Falls River as you walk across a 47 m suspension bridge.
    • \n
    \n
  • Keep an eye out for fur seals/kekeno.
    • \n
    \n
\n

Explore on Google Street View |\xc2\xa0Watch videos\xc2\xa0| Download the free visitor app

\n
\n
\n

Bookings are open for trips from 1 July 2017 \xe2\x80\x93\xc2\xa030 June 2018.

Project Janszoon

\n

Project Janszoon works in partnership with the Department of Conservation to help the ecological restoration of the Abel Tasman National Park.\r\n\r\n

\n\n\n\n

Tonga Island Marine Reserve

\n

Enjoy swimming and snorkelling in the Abel Tasman National Park. It is renowned for its golden sand beaches, intimate coves, and excellent summer weather.

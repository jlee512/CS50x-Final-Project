Heaphy Track

\n
\n

Located in \r\n Kahurangi National Park in \r\n the Nelson/Tasman and West Coast regions\r\n

\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

This track is part of

\n
    \n
  • \n\n\nThis track is one of nine Great Walks - premier tracks that pass through diverse and spectacular scenery.\n\n
    • \n

Introduction

\nTravel through expansive tussock downs, lush forests and n\xc4\xabkau palms to the roaring seas of the West Coast.\n
\n

Highlights

\n
\n
    \n
  • Look and listen out for great spotted kiwi/roroa around Gouland Downs
    • \n
    \n
  • Try and spy the nocturnal carnivorous land snail around Heaphy Hut on a damp night.
    • \n
    \n
  • Get on your bike! Mountain bikes are allowed between 1 May and 30 November.
    • \n
\n

Explore on Google Street View |\xc2\xa0Watch videos

\n
\n
\n

Bookings are open for trips from 1 July 2017 \xe2\x80\x93 30 June 2018.

Track overview

\n
\n
\n

78.4 km

\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Walking and tramping

\n4 - 6 days\nIntermediate: Great Walk/Easier tramping track\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Mountain biking

\n2 - 3 days\nAdvanced: Grade 4
Expert: Grade 5\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Seasonal restrictions

\n

Mountain biking is only allowed from 1 May - 30 November.

\n
\n
\n

Dog access

\n

No dogs

Kahurangi National Park Management Plan amendment

\n

The proposed amendment was to extend the Heaphy Track mountain biking season to 1 April \xe2\x80\x93 30 November, excluding Easter. Submissions closed 12 July 2016.

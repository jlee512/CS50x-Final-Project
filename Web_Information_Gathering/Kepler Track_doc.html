Kepler Track

\n
\n

Located in \r\n Fiordland National Park and Lake Manapouri area and Lake Te Anau area in \r\n the Fiordland region\r\n

\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

This track is part of

\n
    \n
  • \n\n\nThis track is one of nine Great Walks - premier tracks that pass through diverse and spectacular scenery.\n\n
    • \n
  • \n\n\nThis place is part Kiwi Guardians, a conservation programme for Kiwi kids.\n\n

Introduction

\nVast tussock-covered ridgelines and spectacular alpine vistas contrast with peaceful lakeside and valley beech forest in this wilderness adventure.\n
\n

Highlights

\n
\n
    \n
  • Explore the fascinating Luxmore Caves
    • \n
    \n
  • Take in alpine tussock grasslands and views of Lake Te Anau.
    • \n
    \n
  • Walk to Iris Burn Falls
    • \n
\n

Explore on Google Street View |\xc2\xa0Watch videos

\n
\n
\n

Bookings are open for trips from 24 October 2017 \xe2\x80\x93 30 April 2018.

\n

\n

Walking and tramping

\n3-4 days\nIntermediate: Great Walk/Easier tramping track\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Seasonal restrictions

\n
\n

In the Great Walks season (24 October 2017 - 30 April 2018):

\n
\n
    \n
  • Bookings are required for huts and campsites
    • \n
  • Huts have gas cooking stoves and resident wardens
    • \n
\n
\n

Outside the Great Walks season (4 May 2017 - 23 October 2017):

\n
\n
    \n
  • Facilities are greatly reduced
    • \n
  • Avalanche risk exists
    • \n
  • Experience is required
    • \n
  • Bookings are not required - huts and campsites are first come, first served
    • \n
\n

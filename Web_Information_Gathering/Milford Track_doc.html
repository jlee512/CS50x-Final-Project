Introduction

\nExperience \'the finest walk in the world\' as you retrace the steps of early explorers on the world-renowed Milford Track. Take a journey along valleys carved by glaciers, wander through ancient rainforests and admire cascading waterfalls.\n
\n

Highlights

\n
\n
    \n
  • Take your camera and capture the stunning panoramas from Mackinnon Pass across ancient valleys carved by glaciers.
    • \n
    \n
  • Take a side trip to Sutherland Falls, which drop 580 m.
    • \n
    \n
  • Enjoy the emerald waters of the Clinton River.
    • \n
\n

Explore with Google Street View |\xc2\xa0Watch videos

\n
\n
\n

Bookings are open for trips from 24 October 2017 \xe2\x80\x93 30 April 2018.

Track overview

\n
\n
\n

53.5 km one way

\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Walking and tramping

\n4 days\nIntermediate: Great Walk/Easier tramping track\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Seasonal restrictions

\n
\n

In the Great Walks season (24 October 2017 - 30 April 2018):

\n
\n
    \n
  • Bookings are required for huts
    • \n
  • Huts have gas cooking stoves and resident wardens
    • \n
\n
\n

Outside the Great Walks season (5 May 2017 - 23 October 2017):

\n
\n
    \n
  • Facilities, including bridges, are greatly reduced
    • \n
  • Flood and avalanche risks exist
    • \n
  • Experience is required
    • \n
  • Bookings are not required - huts are first come, first served
    • \n
\n
\n
\n

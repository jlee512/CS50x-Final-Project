Routeburn Track

\n
\n

Located in \r\n Fiordland National Park and Mount Aspiring National Park in \r\n the Otago and Fiordland regions\r\n

\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

This track is part of

\n
    \n
  • \n\n\nThis track is one of nine Great Walks - premier tracks that pass through diverse and spectacular scenery.\n\n
    • \n

Introduction

\nThis is the ultimate alpine adventure, weaving through meadows, reflective tarns and alpine gardens. You\'ll be rewarded with spectacular vistas over vast mountain ranges and valleys.\n
\n

Highlights

\n
\n
    \n
  • Wind through the Routeburn gorge along the crystal-clear Route Burn (river).
    • \n
    \n
  • Follow the cascade of Routeburn Falls.
    • \n
    \n
  • Take in spectacular views of the Hollyford valley and Darren Mountains.
    • \n
    \n
  • Walk right past Earland Falls (174 m).
    • \n
    \n
\n

Explore on Google Street View |\xc2\xa0Watch videos

\n
\n
\n

Bookings are open for trips from 24 October 2017 \xe2\x80\x93 30 April 2018.

Track overview

\n
\n
\n

32 km one way

\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Walking and tramping

\n2-4 days\nIntermediate: Great Walk/Easier tramping track\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Seasonal restrictions

\n
\n

In the Great Walks season (24 October 2017 - 30 April 2018):

\n
\n
    \n
  • Bookings are required for huts and campsites
    • \n
  • Huts have gas cooking stoves and resident wardens
    • \n
\n
\n

Outside the Great Walks season (4 May 2017 - 23 October 2017):

\n
\n
    \n
  • Facilities, including bridges, are greatly reduced
    • \n
  • Flood and avalanche risks exist
    • \n
  • Experience is required
    • \n
  • Bookings are not required - huts and campsites are first come, first served
    • \n
\n
\n
\n

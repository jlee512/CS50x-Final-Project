Introduction

\nExplore the volcanic heart of Tongariro National Park, a landscape of stark glacial contrasts and alpine views.\n
\n

Highlights

\n
\n
    \n
  • Journey through dramatic (and active!) volcanic landscapes, glacial valleys, native beech forest, alpine meadows and emerald coloured lakes
    • \n
    \n
  • This path winds its way past Mt Tongariro and Mt Ngauruhoe
    • \n
    \n
  • Explore a World Heritage Area of natural and cultural significance
    • \n
\n

Watch videos

\n
\n
\n

Bookings are open for the trips from 20 October 2017 to 30 April 2018.

Walking and tramping

\n3-4 days\nIntermediate: Great Walk/Easier tramping track\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n

Seasonal restrictions

\n
\n

In the Great Walks season (21 October 2017 - 30 April 2018):

\n
\n
    \n
  • Bookings are required for huts and campsites
    • \n
  • Huts have gas cooking stoves and resident wardens
    • \n
\n
\n

Outside the Great Walks season (1 May 2017 - 20 October 2017):

\n
\n
    \n
  • Facilities are greatly reduced
    • \n
  • Avalanche risk exists
    • \n
  • Experience is required
    • \n
  • Bookings are not required \xe2\x80\x93\xc2\xa0huts and campsites are first come, first served
    • \n
\n
\n
\n

Dog access

\n

No dogs
